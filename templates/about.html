About Loonges

We are a leading innovator in passive cooling technology, dedicated to sustainable and efficient thermal solutions.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Loonges began with a simple mission: to replace single-use cooling waste with durable, reusable, and high-performance alternatives.

Over the past decade, we have grown from a small workshop to a comprehensive manufacturing enterprise. Our team of thermal engineers and production specialists work tirelessly to push the boundaries of what passive cooling can achieve.

Today, our products serve clients in over 30 countries, supporting industries ranging from pharmaceuticals to fresh food e-commerce.
