About Loonges

We are a leading innovator in passive cooling technology, dedicated to sustainable and efficient thermal solutions.

Born from Freshness, Built on Commitment

In 2015, we embarked on our journey with a simple belief: there should be a better bridge between food preservation and environmental protection. We listened intently to users' frustrations with traditional cold chain solutions, from complaints during a camping trip to expectations for fresh ingredients – all becoming the starting point for our research and development.

With meticulous craftsmanship, we reinvented the product: using food-grade materials and a unique multi-chamber structure, we created a soft, foldable, flexible ice pack that provides over 24 hours of preservation and is absolutely safe. It's compact and reliable, integrating technology into every aspect of life.

After ten years of dedicated effort, we have grown into an innovator with over 180 patents and multiple international system certifications, collaborating with national key laboratories to provide reliable support to leading clients in the industry. Today, we are bringing this Chinese ingenuity to the world.

We firmly believe that true preservation begins with caring for products and ends with being gentle to the planet. Flexible ice packs make freshness and sustainability easily accessible.
