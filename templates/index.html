Why Loonges?

With the vision of a more environmentally friendly and efficient cold chain solution, it focuses on green, smart and sustainable, and redefines the cold chain standard.

Advanced Cooling Technology

Equipped with patented cold storage liquid formula, cold storage time exceeds 24 hours, fast cooling speed and stable temperature. The multi-chamber structure allows the cold to be evenly released, greatly improving the preservation and cooling effect, and meeting the long-term low-temperature needs in different scenarios.

Ingenious Design

Food-grade composite PE shell, with double seal + check valve technology, completely eliminates the potential risk of leakage, and can directly contact food and skin. The folding design is thin and portable, and remains soft and tough after freezing, and can be flexibly adapted to various spaces without taking up too much space when stored.

Ecological Advantages

Recyclable, reducing disposable plastic waste from the source, low-carbon and environmentally friendly. The products are non-toxic, harmless and pollution-free, in line with the sustainable development concept of green life and commercial logistics, and support customized sizes and specifications according to customer needs to adapt to diverse usage scenarios.
